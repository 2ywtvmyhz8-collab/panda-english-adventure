🔤 Phonics Lab：Long e

課本 Unit 3：e_e, ee, ea, y, ey。注意：ea 有例外，例如 healthy, bread, weather 不是 long e。

e_e
athlete, complete, these
ee
green, tree, feet, sheep
ea
eat, teacher, beach, read
y / ey
happy, healthy, key, money

Comparatives 比較級
short adjective + er：healthy → healthier
long adjective：more + adjective，例如 more nutritious
be going to
I am going to eat fruit.

They are going to drink water.
